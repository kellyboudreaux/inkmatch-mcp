# InkMatch Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

## Overview

InkMatch ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered tattoo design generator. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use InkMatch through the ChatGPT App Directory.

## Information We Collect

### Within ChatGPT (Preview Generation)
When you use InkMatch within ChatGPT to generate a preview design:
- **Design preferences**: Style, meaning, tone, elements, placement, size, and color preferences you provide
- **Generated images**: Preview images created based on your preferences

We do NOT collect or store:
- Your ChatGPT conversation history
- Your OpenAI account information
- Any personal identifiers

### On InkMatch.io (Full Design Purchase)
If you choose to purchase full designs on inkmatch.io:
- **Email address**: To deliver your designs
- **Payment information**: Processed securely by Stripe via RevenueCat (we never see your card details)
- **Design preferences**: Passed from ChatGPT to personalize your experience

## How We Use Information

- **Generate designs**: Your preferences are used solely to create personalized tattoo designs
- **Deliver purchases**: Your email is used to send your purchased designs
- **Improve the service**: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be used to improve design quality

## Data Retention

- **Preview images**: Not retained after your ChatGPT session ends
- **Purchase data**: Email and designs retained to fulfill orders and provide customer support
- **Payment records**: Retained as required by law and payment processor requirements

## Third-Party Services

InkMatch uses the following third-party services:
- **Replicate**: AI image generation (their privacy policy: https://replicate.com/privacy)
- **RevenueCat/Stripe**: Payment processing (their privacy policies apply to payment data)
- **Resend**: Email delivery (for purchased design delivery)

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure API key management
- No storage of sensitive payment information

## Your Rights

You may:
- Request deletion of your purchase data by emailing support@inkmatch.io
- Opt out of promotional emails (we only send transactional emails for purchases)

## Children's Privacy

InkMatch is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy questions or concerns:
- Email: support@inkmatch.io
- Website: https://inkmatch.io

## ChatGPT App Directory Compliance

This app complies with OpenAI's App Directory policies. InkMatch:
- Does not store ChatGPT conversation data
- Does not use conversation data for training
- Only collects information necessary to provide the service
- Provides clear disclosure of data practices
